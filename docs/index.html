Clinical Graph Pipeline Architecture

Click through the modular stages to inspect data transformation from raw EHR to sequence predictions.

STAGE 01

MIMIC-IV Preprocessing

Cleaning, filtering, and standardizing raw admissions, lab events, and prescriptions.

STAGE 02

Neo4j Graph Construction

Ingesting patient entities and enriching with external drug-drug & disease ontologies.

STAGE 03

GAT Representation Learning

Computing Graph Attention Network embeddings for semantic entity context.

STAGE 04

Multi-Task Sequence Models

Temporal alignment into patient timelines for downstream RNN/Transformer prediction.

STAGE 01

MIMIC-IV Preprocessing & Ontological Standardizing

Parses raw relational EHR tables from MIMIC-IV. Applies medical filtering, handles missing clinical values, and aligns ICD diagnosis codes with external medical ontologies.
